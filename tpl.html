Goku is a Pokémon Ranger living in Hau'oli City. Originally from Safrania (Saffron City), she moved to the beautiful region of Alola after graduating from the Rangers school of Acacia (Harmony Region).

Outdoorsy and adventurous, diving missions and ocean rescues are her main focus at the job. But she enjoys a good surf or a relaxing day at the beach on her days off.

She used to be reserved and anxious during her teen years, but became friendly and more confident after school graduation. She's a very loyal and caring friend, yet she can also be very short-tempered, competitive and fiercely protective.

SUMMARY

Goku grew up in Safrania (Safron City), where she was raised by her father (Ken), a Pokémon professor and researcher. As a kid she was really admirative of her local gym leader: Sabrina and dreamt of becoming a gym leader herself someday.

Her mom (Miki) is a famous Pokémon Performer in Hoenn region. Goku admires her, yet knows very little about her as she's always been away, dedicated to her career. As a young adult Goku resents her a bit for her lack of presence...

Following her father on the field, her journey took her to the Orange archipelago where she met Jacky (Kenji) an aspiring Pokémon Watcher. At his sides, she learnt to care for Pokémons outside of an arena, and that's when sparkled the idea of becoming a Pokémon Ranger.

To follow her dream she attended the Ranger school of Acacia in Harmony. Despite being used to travel and discovering new places, it was her very first time completely on her own. The loneliness brought her some anxiety, though, thankfully she met classmates Binka and Sarha that she befriended and soon enough the trio became best friends.
During those school years, she kept contact with her friend Jacky and the two of them started dating. Even if they could only see each other on school breaks, their teen crush on each other grew into a tender love story.

She participated in several rookie to advanced missions with her friends. One of them in particular became a life changer for her as she had a furtive encounter with the Legendary Guardian of the Seas: Lugia. She only kept a feather dropped by the Pokémon, as a memory of this special moment.

After graduation, Goku moved back with her father (and Jacky) in Hau'oli City. She is now a certified Ranger and goes on regular missions around Alola to help both people and Pokémons in need. Being friendly and caring, she acquired a reliable and trustworthy reputation among Alolans.

Goku and Jacky participate in surfing tournaments regularly, as Goku is really competitive, she enjoys some challenges even with her dear partner!
